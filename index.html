hardware tester

an all-in-one web tool to diagnose and verify your device's hardware functionality.

dead pixel test

click start to enter full screen. use your arrow keys or spacebar to cycle through various colors to spot any stuck or dead pixels. press esc to exit.

keyboard & touchpad test

press the physical keys on your keyboard and click your touchpad buttons to ensure they register correctly. note: system shortcuts like the windows key will still open os menus.

battery health

browsers cannot read raw battery data. open command prompt, type powercfg /batteryreport, and paste the generated file path into your browser to view the report.

formula: (full charge capacity / design capacity) * 100

0-50: defective  |  51-69: minimal  |  70-100: good

storage health

browsers cannot read raw smart disk data. to test your storage drive health, use a dedicated diagnostic tool.

camera test

select your webcam from the dropdown and click enable. confirm the video feed displays clearly without flickering or dead zones.

microphone test

choose your microphone, click record, and speak clearly for a few seconds. the visualizer will react to your voice. click stop, then play the audio back.

speaker test
